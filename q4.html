Do we pay too much or too little of our income in taxes?

Governments rely on taxation (income, property, and sales) as a major source of revenue. Public policy influences and regulates financial incentives (buying a home) and disincentives (spending retirement savings before retirement age)

Gideon v. Wainwright - 1964

The Supreme Court ruled that the Constitution requires the states to provide defense attorneys to criminal defendants charged with serious offenses who cannot afford lawyers themselves.
