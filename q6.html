How does informed citizenship support democratic government?

13th, 14th, and 15th Reconstruction Amendment

13th Amendment - Bans slavery in U.S. states and territories. 14th Amendment - Defines citizenship and grants equal protection under the law for all citizens. 15th Amendment - Gives citizens the right to vote regardless of race, color or previous condition of servitude.

Impartiality of Justices

Impartiality is a principle of justice holding that decisions should be based on objective criteria, rather than on the basis of bias, prejudice, or preferring the benefit to one person over another for improper reasons.
